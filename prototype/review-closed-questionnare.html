Purpose and General Scope of agreement
This Nonreimbursable Space Act Agreement ’shall be for the purpose of collaborative research to study attentional tunneling, otherwise known as attentional cognitive fixation. This is a well-known human factors safety problem in the context of advanced aeronautic displays. NASA and UCSD propose to gather research data in NASA ARC’s Advanced Controls and Displays (ACD) laboratory utilizing Calit2’s Sound Bender system prototype, along with NASA’s Auditory Alert System.
